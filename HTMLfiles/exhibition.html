Art Exhibitions


We are an online art gallery! To give you the best options to experience art we annually organize art exhibitions, pop-up art galleries and open art showrooms at Dehiwala-Mount Lavinia, Colombo.
The annual LOTUS art exhibition holds nearly July, at our showroom where Dehiwala-Mount Lavinia. Not only we show the historical artworks, but also we organize exhibitions for our nation artists to express their art talent.
The purpose of these exhibitions is unique and driven by our artists' ideas, the context of space, season and the vibes of the region. We arrange these types of exhibitions in Jaffna, Kandy, Galle and also in Colombo, by the request of private artists, group of artists or organizations.

LOTUS Art Gallery

Lotus art gallery is an online gallery focused on highlighting the best contemporary art. Lotus offers consultancy services to both private and corporate clients, working with private collectors and with interior designers, architects and property developers. Lotus provides end-to-end advisory service, from developing art themes and narratives, concept design development, and the commissioning of artworks to project and site specifications.
